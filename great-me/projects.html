Project 1

Tech Education Website

I created a web page where learners who are ambitious about learning tech courses can apply, learn, and receive certificates upon completion.

The website contains different pages, such as:

  • Login Page: Allows learners to log into the website.
  • Registration Page: Enables new learners to sign up and access available courses.
  • Course Catalog Page: Displays available courses and their durations.
  • Course Details Page: Provides descriptions and contents of each course.
  • Certification Page: Displays and allows learners to download awarded certificates.
  • Feedback Page: Allows learners to provide feedback on courses and the website.

This ensures easy navigation and accessibility of website features.

Project 2

Tech Job Portal

To help unemployed tech professionals connect with employers, I created a job portal where companies, mentors, and job seekers can interact and apply for jobs.

The website contains different pages, such as:

  1. Registration Page: Allows new users to create an account and access job listings and mentorship.
  2. Login Page: Enables users to log into their accounts.
  3. Job Listing Page: Displays job postings, descriptions, and application instructions.
  4. Mentorship Page: Connects learners with experienced professionals for career guidance.
  5. User Profile Page: Allows users to showcase their skills, experience, and achievements.
  6. Job Application Page: Enables job seekers to apply directly to job posts.
  7. Community Forum/Chat Page: Provides a space for discussions, knowledge sharing, and direct communication.
  8. Job Category Page: Organizes jobs into categories for easy browsing.
  9. Collaboration Page: Allows users to collaborate on tech projects and share ideas.
